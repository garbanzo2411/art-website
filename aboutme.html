About Me

Hello! My name is Matea Mihaljević and I am an artist based in Salt Lake City, Utah. My journey as an artist has been deeply influenced by my cultural heritage and the stories that shaped my family's history.

My art mainly focuses on the country of Bosnia and the struggles of its people. Through my paintings, I aim to capture not just the visual beauty of the region, but also the resilience, hope, and complex emotions that define the Bosnian experience.

Each piece I create is a bridge between past and present, between personal memory and collective history. I believe art has the power to heal, to remember, and to connect us across cultures and generations.
